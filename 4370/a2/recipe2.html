Black Bean Lasagna

Recipe
Recipe #2

Ingredients

  • 2 16-oz. cans black beans, rinsed, drained and slightly mashed
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 2 cups low-fat meatless spaghetti sauce
  • 1 tsp. bottled minced garlic
  • 1 tsp. cumin
  • 15 oz. container low-fat ricotta cheese
  • 1/3 cup grated or shredded parmesan or romano cheese
  • 1/4 cup skim milk
  • 9 cooked lasagna noodles
  • 1 cup shredded low-fat Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 cup shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together beans, salsa, spaghetti sauce, garlic and cumin. Set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl, stir together ricotta, parmesan and milk.
  4. Spray a 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking pan or glass baking dish with vegetable oil spray.
  5. To assemble lasagna:
    1. Place three lasagna noodles on bottom of dish.
    2. Spread 1/3 of bean mixture on the noodles in the dish, making sure that the mixture covers the noodles completely.
    3. Then spread 1/3 of the cheese mixture over that.
    4. Sprinkle with 1/3 of the Monterey Jack cheese and 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese.
    5. Repeat procedure twice so that you have three layers.
  6. Cover dish tightly with foil and bake 30 minutes.
  7. Uncover and bake 10 minutes more or until lasagna is heated through.
  8. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
